How to Play

Controls: Touch and drag your finger to control the dot.

  • X-axis (left/right): Controls your horizontal position
  • Y-axis (up/down): Controls your speed
    • Move finger UP = faster
    • Move finger DOWN = slower

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my nickname?
No, nicknames are permanent once set. To use a different nickname, you must delete all your data in Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Delete All My Data.

How do ghost replays work?
In Race and Fixed modes, you race against recordings of other players' runs. They appear as colored dots with their nicknames.

What are the run limits?
Free users get 3 runs per mode per day (resets at midnight UTC). Unlock unlimited runs with a one-time purchase.

How does the ranking system work?
In Race Mode, you earn or lose ranking points based on your finish position. Top performers gain points, bottom performers lose points. Your rating is displayed in the All-Time Rankings leaderboard.

How do I delete my data?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Delete All My Data. This removes all your scores, ghosts, stats, and nickname from CloudKit.

Troubleshooting

My scores aren't appearing on the leaderboard
Make sure you have:

  • Set a nickname (required for leaderboard submission)
  • Internet connection (scores are stored in CloudKit)
  • Completed the race/run (crashed runs don't always save in endless modes)
Try refreshing the leaderboard with the refresh button.

Ghost players are appearing incorrectly
This is a known issue that has been fixed in the latest version. Please update to the newest version of the app.
